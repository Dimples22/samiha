How You Can Help the Planet

Published Jan, 15 2025

Introduction

The Climate Clock is ticking. Every choice matters, and one area where we can act immediately is plastic pollution, which harms oceans, soil, and wildlife while contributing to climate change.

New Alternatives to Plastic

  • Vivomer - a biodegradable, plant-based material from Shellworks. It can be shaped like plastic and composts naturally. Already used in beauty and cosmetic packaging.
  • Mycelium packaging - made from mushroom roots, fully compostable, used for shipping and protective packaging.
  • Notpla - made from seaweed, used for films, sachets, and disposable packaging.
  • Mango Materials - turns methane gas into biodegradable polymers.

These materials reduce the need for fossil-fuel plastics and help prevent long-term pollution.

How You Can Make a Difference

Support products using sustainable materials

Buy from brands using Vivomer, mycelium, or other biodegradable packaging. Every purchase signals demand for sustainable alternatives.

Advocate to businesses

Suggest alternatives like Vivomer, Notpla, or mycelium to local or global companies. Small recommendations can influence supply chains and encourage widespread adoption.

Sign petitions and push for policy change

Support petitions targeting governments or regulators to ban harmful single-use plastics or require eco-friendly materials. Target decision-makers like the UK government, EU Commission, or local councils.

Reduce personal plastic use

  • Use reusable bottles, bags, and containers.
  • Avoid excessive packaging and single-use items.

Spread awareness

Switching to alternative materials alone will not solve climate change, but every action counts. One purchase, one recommendation, or one message at a time can make a difference!
